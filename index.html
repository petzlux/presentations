About us

Administration du Cadastre et de la Topographie (ACT)

  • ACT dependant of the Ministry of Finance
  • 130 staff

Responsibilities

  • Cadastral documentation & delimitation of properties
  • Administrative & international boundaries
  • National address register
  • Property and topographic geodatabases & maps
  • Geodetic Networks & Diff. GPS network

map.geoportail.lu

Main Geoportal Interface, with a range of functionalities

  • Over 200 different layers of geodata
  • Fast and easy navigation (Google Maps style interface)
  • Single Search for addresses, parcels, localities ...
  • MyMaps functionality for user contributed content
  • Thematic portals for specific topics (tourism, water, environment, agriculture, transport (work in progress) ...)

INSPIRE

EU Directive for a pan-european Spatial Data Infrastructure.

  • Standardised discovery, view and download services for geodata
  • Geoportail.lu is Contact Point and de-facto Luxembourg SDI
  • INSPIRE discovery and view services: inspire.geoportal.lu

api.geoportail.lu

A geo-web services platform

Developer Services on offer

  • GeoAPI: Javascript Mapping API with 100's of geodata layers
  • POI Manager: web-based Points of Interest data & manager
  • Location search, Routing, Geocoding & Reverse Geocoding services
  • WMS & WFS webservices
  • Customised geodata licensing & services

External Data Code

var kmlTroncons = new OpenLayers.Layer.Vector("Troncons de Route", {
	projection: new OpenLayers.Projection("EPSG:4326"),
	strategies: [new OpenLayers.Strategy.Fixed()],
	protocol: new OpenLayers.Protocol.HTTP({
		url: "troncons.kml?t=",
		format: new OpenLayers.Format.KML({
			extractStyles: true, 
			extractAttributes: true,
		})
	})
});
kmlTroncons.bodid = kmlTroncons.id;
geo.map.addLayer(kmlTroncons);
