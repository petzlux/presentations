About us

Administration du Cadastre et de la Topographie (ACT)

  • ACT dependant of the Ministry of Finance
  • 130 staff
  • Geoportail.lu team part of the ACT

Responsibilities

  • Cadastral documentation & delimitation of properties
  • Administrative & international boundaries
  • National address register
  • Property and topographic geodatabases & maps
  • Geodetic Networks & Diff. GPS network
  • Management of the national geoportal: Geoportail.lu

api.geoportail.lu

A geo-web services platform

Developer Services on offer

  • GeoAPI: Javascript Mapping API with 100's of geodata layers
  • POI Manager: web-based Points of Interest data & manager
  • Location search, Geocoding & Reverse Geocoding services
  • WMS & WFS webservices
  • Customised geodata licensing & services
